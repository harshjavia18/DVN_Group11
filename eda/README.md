# EDA — The Bond Penalty

Exploratory data analysis of the NSW rental crisis using NSW Fair Trading bond data (2025) and ABS SEIFA 2021.

## Files
- `EDA_notebook.ipynb` — 8 figures with explanations, runs cell-by-cell
- `INSIGHTS_HANDOVER.docx` — story, persona, and brief for the Tableau team

## Headline finding
Renters in NSW's most disadvantaged postcodes lose **37%** of their bond on average. In the wealthiest postcodes, they lose just **13%**. Same legal system, nearly 3× the loss rate.
